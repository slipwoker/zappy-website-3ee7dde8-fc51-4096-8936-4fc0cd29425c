    • About Noam Med-Defense  Your  Medical Device Expert

    30 Years of Experience, Proven Expertise, and Commitment to Client Success

    I am Noam Avigdor, an engineer with over 30 years of technical and operational experience in the medical device industry and defense industry. 

    At Noam Med-Defense  I provide end-to-end engineering support, specializing in Design Transfer Sustaining Engineering, and process optimization in stringent regulatory environments.

    The core value I bring is a deep and uncompromising connection between Engineering, QA, RA and Manufacturing    Instead of "throwing" recommendations from the outside

    I dive into the details:  BOM, drawings, ECOs, product files, manufacturing and testing processes. My approach is hands-on, based on field experience, and results-oriented.

    I work with a true Risk-Based approach: 

    Focusing on what threatens regulatory approval, product quality or manufacturing stability, sharp distinction between Must and Nice to Have   and making decisions under time, budget and schedule constraints. 

    Over the years I have led process optimization around PLM systems, configuration management and engineering changes

    As well as accelerating approval processes through parallel execution of Design Approval and Process Validation 

    My goal is simple: 

    To help organizations bring products to market faster, in a stable, practical and standards-compliant manner, while reducing risks and increasing operational maturity over time

    Our Story - From Vision to Reality

    Noam Med-Defense was founded from a deep understanding of the challenges medical device companies face on their path to launching new products and obtaining regulatory approvals. As an NPI expert in medical devices, we have seen firsthand how many companies encounter difficulties in managing complex processes, meeting changing regulatory requirements, and efficiently transitioning from development to manufacturing.

    Our vision was clear from the beginning: to create an engineering support and consulting company that would be a true partner to its clients, deeply understand the unique challenges of each project, and provide practical and effective solutions. 
    Transition from development to manufacturing, changes to existing products, and product stabilization under regulatory and business pressure.
    Connection between engineering, quality and regulation,

    In-depth product involvement, while making Risk-Based  decisions for process and production line improvement until actual stabilization. I accompany medical device companies precisely at the points where things tend to get stuck:
    And actually connect between Engineering, QA, RA and manufacturing not at the presentation level, but at the BOM, drawing and production line level.

    Our Areas of Expertise

    As an NPI expert in medical devices and an expert in design transfer from development to manufacturing in medical device systems, covering the entire spectrum of medical device development, regulation and manufacturing processes. Broad specialization across a wide range of device types and markets.

    Device Types

    We work with a wide range of medical devices - active and passive devices, in-vitro diagnostic devices, implantable devices, digital devices and general medical equipment. Each type of device has unique regulatory requirements, and we know them in depth.

    Regulatory Markets

    We specialize in working with leading health authorities worldwide - FDA (United States), CE (Europe), Health Canada and others. We know the specific requirements of each authority and the required processes.

    Standards and Regulations

    We are proficient in all standards and regulations relevant to the medical device industry, including ISO 13485, ISO 14971 (Risk Management), 21 CFR Part 820 (QSR), MDR (European Medical Device Regulations), and more.

    Development Phases

    Accompanying companies through all development phases: from initial concept, through concept development, prototype, clinical trials, transfer to manufacturing and up to marketing and post-launch support. Our experience allows us to identify challenges in advance and plan accordingly.
